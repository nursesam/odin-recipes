Tilapia Fish Tacos

Description

This recipe will show you how to prepare fish to use in fish tacos. It uses a couple of indgredients you probably already have and is a very economical way to make fish tacos. I eat them with shredded cheese, lettuce, and salsa. Prepare them however you like and enjoy.

Indgredients:

  • 16oz pack of boneless, skinless tilapia fillets
  • 1 packet of taco seasoning
  • 1 bag of plain, salted tortilla chips
  • Taco shells of your choosing
  • Toppings for your tacos

Steps:

  1. Cut thawed tilapia fillets into small cubes no greater than 1"
  2. Heat oven to 375
  3. In a 1 gallon ziplock bag use a smooth sided mug or rolling pin to crush about 30-40 of the tortilla chips inside the bag
  4. Mix in the taco seasoning
  5. Place the cut fish into the bag a few at a time, manipulating them to ensure even coating and preventing them from sticking together
  6. Close and shake the bag
  7. Line a baking sheet with aluminum foil. Spray with non-stick baking oil
  8. Pour fish mixture onto the prepared baking sheet
  9. Cook for 8 minutes, flip the fish, then cook for another 7 minutes
  10. Check a few of the biggest pieces of fish for to make sure they are finished cooking by ensuring the fish is flaky when cut in half
  11. Serve tacos with whatever toppings you like

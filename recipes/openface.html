Cheeseburgers

This is a recipe for how to make turkey burgers. It just uses 3 indgredients. I get turkey rolls from Aldis. I make 4 burgers out of each 1 lb roll so get as much as you will need.

Indgredients:

  • 1 lb roll of ground turkey
  • salt
  • pepper
  • cheese and other burger toppings of your choice
  • buns of your choice

Steps:

  1. Separate ground turkey into 4 equal parts. Manipulate into thin burgers. Press a hole in the center with your finger. Tip: Only manipulate the meat as much as needed to get the proper shape. It will taste better if it isn't mashed much.
  2. Salt and pepper each side of patties to taste
  3. Cook on heated grill. Flip once. Use a meat thermometer to determine when done.
  4. Serve and enjoy.
